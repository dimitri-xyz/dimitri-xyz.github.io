The sum \(S\) of the geometric progression with \(n\) terms, ratio \(r\) and first term \(a\) \[ S = a + a r + a r^2 + a r^3 + \ldots + a r^{n-2} + a r^{n-1} \] is calculated buy multiplying the equation above by \(r\) to obtain \[ r S = a r + a r^2 + a r^3 + \ldots + a r^{n-1} + a r^n \] And then subtracting the top equation from the bottom one to obtain \[ \begin{eqnarray*} r S - S & = & \;\;\;\;\; a r \;+ a r^2 \;+ a r^3 \;+ \ldots \;+ a r^{n-1} \;+ a r^n \\ & & - a - a r - a r^2 - a r^3 - \ldots - a r^{n-1} \end{eqnarray*} \] On the right, all but two terms cancel out and we get \[ \begin{eqnarray*} r S - S & = & a r^n - a \\ S ( r - 1 ) & = & a ( r^n - 1 ) \\ & & \\ S & = & a \frac{r^n - 1}{r-1} \end{eqnarray*} \]
